Traffic Congestion

Daily gridlocks and slow-moving traffic are a major challenge in our city. Overcrowded roads, inefficient signals, and limited public transport options lead to wasted time and increased air pollution.

  • Rush hour gridlocks on main roads
  • Long commute times and frustration for residents
  • Increased vehicle emissions harming air quality
  • Need for smarter, tech-driven traffic management
