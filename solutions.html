Tech-Driven Solutions

Smart Traffic Lights

Adaptive traffic signals use real-time data from sensors and cameras to optimize flow, reduce congestion, and lower emissions. Mobile apps can alert drivers to the best routes.

IoT-Enabled Waste Bins

Smart bins with fill-level sensors notify collection teams, optimize routes, and encourage recycling through digital incentives and community challenges.

Urban Air Quality Sensors

A citywide network of sensors provides real-time air quality data to citizens and authorities, helping to target pollution sources and inform public health decisions.

Renewable Energy Monitoring

Solar panels and smart meters track energy generation and usage, empowering residents to save energy and reduce the city's carbon footprint.

Urban Farming Apps

Mobile platforms connect urban gardeners, provide tips, and map available green spaces, fostering community-driven food production and sustainability.
